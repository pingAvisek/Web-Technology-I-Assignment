BIT INFONEPAL || bitinfoNepal

bitinfonepal.com is a startup project that focuses on assisting Bachelor in Information & Technology(BIT) students in preparing for their examinations by providing past questions, BIT filtered notices,micro-syllabus, notes, and many other resources.

Any student enrolled in a Bachelor of Information & Technology program in Nepal can useBitinfonepal.com to gain access to useful study resources.
